My take

Three judgement calls based on how each variant survives at favicon size and how well it tells your story.

KEEP if… the F-mark is yours.
If "Fluentra" being F-shaped is intentional brand DNA, the current mark wins. It's the only option that doubles as a wordmark — readable from a tab bar without "Fluentra" next to it.
SWAP to #02 (brackets) if you want meaning.
Translation brackets [ ] with a center dot tells the bilingual story without needing language. Holds up at 14px. My pick if you want the inside to mean something specific to a language app.
SKIP #06 / #05 — they're too generic.
Globe and book are what every other language app already uses (Duolingo, Babbel, Rosetta). Picking either makes you look me-too instead of distinct. Save the differentiation for the inside.
